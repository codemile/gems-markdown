[![Feed-tw][3]][2][![Feed-fb][5]][4][Fashion Week][6] returned to New York City Thursday, and attendees were ready for the action, iPhones in hand (see: photo three).

At Lincoln Center, Nicholas K kicked off the week with a 9 a.m. showing of his Spring/Summer 2014 collection, an urban interpretation of Native American dress in light, neutral-colored fabrics. The day continued with shows for David Tlale, Bcbgmaxazria, Richard Chai, Supima, Tadashi Shoji, Candela, Desigual (pictured above), Honor, Marissa Webb and the Art Institute of New York City. Downtown, Titania Inglis and Costello Tagliapietra debuted their spring/summer 2014 collections at The Standard and Milk Studios, respectively. [Read more...][7]
