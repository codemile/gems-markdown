![Bill-gates-worlds-richest-man][1][![Feed-tw][3]][3][![Feed-fb][5]][5]Thanks to a recent stock surge, Microsoft co-founder Bill Gates is once again the world's richest man, a title he hasn't held since 2007, according to Bloomberg.

Gates' fortune is now worth $72.1 billion, according to [Bloomberg][6], putting him about $550 million ahead of the former No. 1, Mexico's Carlos Slim. Gates' wealth has jumped 10% this year as Microsoft's shares have risen

On Thursday, the company's stock price hit a five-year high. Slim's wealth has fallen $2 billion this year as Mexico's Congress passed a monopoly-busting telecom bill that threatens his company America Movil SAB's commanding position in the market. [Read more...][7]
